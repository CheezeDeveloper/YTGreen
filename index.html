Features
What you get
No feature bloat. Just the stuff you actually use.

Paste & Download

Paste a link, pick a quality, hit download. No sign-ups, no waiting.

Queue System

Stack up multiple downloads. They run one after another automatically.

Batch Mode

Dump a list of URLs. Fetch all their info at once. Add them all to queue.

Video Preview

See the thumbnail, title, channel, and duration before you download anything.

Trim Clips

Set a start and end time. Download only the segment you actually want.

Audio Only

Extract audio as MP3 or M4A. Good for music, podcasts, whatever.

History

Every download is logged. Redownload anything with one click.

.YTG files

A file format made for YTGreen. Cache video metadata and thumbnails into a single file. Hand it to someone, they open it, and every video loads into their queue instantly. No refetching.

  • 1 Fetch info for one or more videos
  • 2 Hit "Save .YTG"
  • 3 Send the file to whoever you want
  • 4 They open it — instant queue, zero wait
